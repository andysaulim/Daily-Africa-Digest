DRC's Ebola outbreak hits 676 confirmed cases one month after declaration, with no containment in sight. WHO data cited by Africanews puts the outbreak at 676 confirmed cases and 136 deaths since May 15. Funding shortfalls, active conflict zones, and disinformation are hampering response. AllAfrica's June 13 dispatch frames the Sudan conflict as the war the world chose to forget — suffering undiminished, peace absent, humanitarian catastrophe unresolved, yet no longer commanding consistent international coverage. The PRESS DELTA framing divergence is live here: African outlets name UAE as RSF's primary backer with defector accounts as evidence; Western outlets treat UAE attribution as Khartoum's claim and hedge accordingly. The war now enters its fourth year with no active peace track commanding major-power attention. Watch: whether the AU or any third party moves to revive a mediation framework.
