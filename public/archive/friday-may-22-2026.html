India canceled the first India-Africa summit in over a decade, set for next week in Delhi, citing the Bundibugyo Ebola outbreak in eastern DRC (BBC). US Congress Pushes Bipartisan Bill to Designate RSF a Terrorist Organization
A bipartisan congressional bill recognizes genocide in Darfur and calls for the RSF's formal designation as a terrorist organization, per Ayin Network. The legislation opens a complex debate over humanitarian access — aid groups fear designation could restrict relief corridors. RSF adviser El Basha Tebeig separately threatened to cut oil flows if SAF drone strikes on RSF positions continue (Dabanga). LIBERIA
Boakai Sends War Crimes Court and Anti-Corruption Court Bills to Legislature
President Boakai formally submitted legislation to the House of Representatives to establish a War and Economic Crimes Court and a National Anti-Corruption Court, per the Liberian Investigator. Supporters call it the most consequential accountability push since the end of Liberia's civil wars. The bills face a legislature with mixed appetite for retrospective justice. Watch: committee scheduling and civil-society mobilization.
