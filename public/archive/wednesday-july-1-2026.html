Anti-immigration marches across South Africa triggered a multi-country evacuation scramble. The vote formalizes an institutional contest between two figures who ran on the same 2024 ticket. Analysts read it through a sovereignty-and-succession lens rather than instability. Watch whether Faye contests implementation and how PASTEF's internal factions align.

Watch OCHA access figures and any ICC referral movement.
