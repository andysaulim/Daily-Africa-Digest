RSF masses near El Obeid as SAF downs Tawila drone: Seven European states and the African Commission warned of an imminent RSF offensive on El Obeid in North Kordofan, RFI reported June 24, with the NYT noting the city's strategic Darfur-to-east highway. The dominant eastern DRC development is the Ebola outbreak, which UN humanitarians say has spread at unprecedented speed, topping 1,000 cases and 250 deaths, complicating FARDC and humanitarian operations across the conflict zone, per UN News June 24.

Ebola outbreak strains eastern DRC response: The WHO and US officials confirmed the US is releasing experimental Ebola treatment doses for clinical trials as the outbreak widens, RFI reported June 24. The NYT reported symptoms in the current outbreak may be milder than in previous ones — better for patients but, officials fear, harder to control because cases are less obvious, raising transmission risk across the displacement-heavy east.
