The UN singles out both Sudan belligerents for systematic abuse and flags growing drone warfare. The embassy opening follows Israel's recognition of Somaliland's independence six months ago — the first sovereign recognition Somaliland has received from any state. The OIC called the move illegal and a violation of international law and the UN Charter, reaffirming support for Somalia's territorial integrity. The BBC confirmed the opening, framing it as a direct product of the bilateral recognition deal. Watch: whether Mogadishu escalates diplomatic countermeasures and whether other states move toward engagement. (Shabelle/BBC, Jun 16)

Nigeria's central bank launches the Nigeria Overnight Financing Rate as opposition parties face existential court order. CBN Governor Olayemi Cardoso unveiled NOFR to deepen FX market stability and strengthen price discovery. Simultaneously, a Federal High Court ordered INEC to deregister the ADC and four other opposition parties — directly threatening former VP Atiku Abubakar's 2027 presidential bid and Osun Governor Adeleke's re-election ticket. BudgIT flagged the 2026 federal budget as unviable: N68.3 trillion in expenditure against N36.9 trillion revenue yields a N31.5 trillion deficit (6.4% of GDP).
