The Namibian police angle—reportedly central to Ramaphosa's evidentiary strategy—adds a cross-border dimension to what began as a domestic livestock-sale scandal. Sudan's West Kordofan cholera outbreak kills 40 and infects 228 as measles spreads in East Darfur refugee camps. Dabanga reported that health authorities in West Kordofan confirmed the death toll and case count, while emergency teams in East Darfur warned of rising suspected measles cases in a refugee camp population. Sudan's Finance Minister simultaneously led a delegation to the AfDB Annual Meetings in Brazzaville, signaling continued engagement with multilateral creditors even as the humanitarian crisis deepens in conflict-affected states. The co-occurrence of two separate disease outbreaks in SAF-administered territory reflects collapsing public health infrastructure. Watch: WHO response capacity and RSF vs. SAF access dynamics in affected areas. (Dabanga, May 27; SNA, May 27)
